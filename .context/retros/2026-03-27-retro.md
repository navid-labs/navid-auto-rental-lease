### Where to Level Up

1. **Test coverage needs investment.** At 3.9% test LOC ratio this week, most new features shipped with scaffold tests (render-only) rather than behavioral tests. For reviewability and rollback safety, consider splitting future large migrations into atomic steps: (1) add new infrastructure, (2) migrate feature-by-feature, (3) remove old code. Even in a solo project, this improves git bisect debugging.
