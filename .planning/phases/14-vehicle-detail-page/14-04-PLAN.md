      - Extended warranty: from manufacturer warranty end to `warrantyEndDate`
      - Progress value: percentage elapsed
      - Label: "연장 보증" with date range
    - **Remaining info text**:
      - "남은 기간: {months}개월" (calculated from today to warrantyEndDate or manufacturer end)

    - **Visual**: Two progress bars stacked vertically with labels and date ranges
    - If warranty expired: show "보증 만료" badge in destructive color
    - Container: `<Card className="p-6">`
